How it`s made?

  • We are able to produce up to 8,500 ice creams per hour on just one production line. 75,000 per day. But it’s not always a challenge, ice cream is a seasonal product.

  • The factory can handle up to 100 tons of milk. The whole milk brought from the farms is first delivered to the milk dispenser, from where it is sent to the laboratory for control. If the milk is all right, it will be pasteurized and then it will be stored in a giant tank or immediately put into production.

  • The fatness of the milk may vary at the time of acceptance, in fact it is an optional criterion in the production of ice cream. It is important that the finished filling has a fat value of 12% - that is the requirement. To get this percentage of fat, the normalized milk is combined with condensed and cream. And if the milk analyzer tests show that the mixture does not reach the required fat, you can add some butter to the future filling.
